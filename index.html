Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify a therapist's credentials?

expand_more
Every therapist on our platform must provide proof of professional qualification and insurance. You can view these verification badges directly on their individual profile pages. We conduct annual audits to ensure all documents remain current.

Can I book for someone else?

expand_more
Yes, you can purchase gift vouchers or book on behalf of others. Simply specify the recipient's details during the checkout process to ensure the therapist has the correct medical history for the attendee.

What is the cancellation policy?

expand_more
Standard policies allow for full refunds up to 24 hours before your appointment. Some therapists may have specific terms which are clearly displayed on their booking page during the reservation process.
