Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How do I verify a therapist's credentials?
Every therapist on the directory must provide proof of professional qualification and insurance before their profile goes live. You'll see verification badges on each profile, and we re-check documents annually to make sure they stay current.
Can I book for someone else?
Yes — you can buy gift vouchers or book on someone else's behalf. Add the recipient's details at checkout so the therapist has the right health information for the person attending.
What is the cancellation policy?
Most bookings can be cancelled with a full refund up to 24 hours before your appointment. Some therapists set their own terms — these are shown clearly on their booking page before you confirm.
